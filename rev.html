Biography

Reverend Passy Kahindo is a devoted servant of God and missionary from the Democratic Republic of Congo. From a young age, he felt the calling to serve God and spread the Gospel. Through prayer and dedication, he entered ministry and began preaching the Word of God across communities.

His missionary journey brought him to Kenya where he continues to impact lives through evangelism, teaching, and community service. Reverend Passy is passionate about youth empowerment, discipleship, and spreading hope through Christ. Many people have been transformed through his ministry, and he continues to serve faithfully with humility and love.
